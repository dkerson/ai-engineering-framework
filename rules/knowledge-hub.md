# Knowledge Hub

## Regras

- Consultar apenas areas relevantes em `knowledge/`.
- Registrar areas consultadas no Working Context.
- Nao copiar documentacao externa.
- Nao colar documentos inteiros em respostas internas ou finais.
- Knowledge Hub informa decisoes, mas nao substitui contexto do projeto.

## Roteamento minimo

- Landing page: copywriting, branding, seo, marketing, ux, design
- Dashboard: dashboard, analytics, ux, design-system, performance
- Mobile app: mobile, accessibility, performance, design-system
- Backend/API: backend, architecture, security, performance
